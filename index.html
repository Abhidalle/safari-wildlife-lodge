Welcome to Safari Wildlife Lodge and Camp

Welcome to Hotel Safari, your home away from home in the heart of Sauraha, Chitwan. Just minutes from Chitwan National Park, our family-run hotel offers peaceful rooms, local hospitality, and unforgettable jungle experiences. Whether you're here for adventure or relaxation, we’re here to make your stay comfortable, authentic, and memorable.

We Offer

Hotel Room

At Hotel Safari, we offer cozy, well-furnished rooms designed for both comfort and convenience. Each room comes equipped with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, clean linens, and private bathrooms to make your stay relaxing. Whether you're a solo traveler or with family, our rooms provide the perfect retreat after a day of exploring Sauraha and Chitwan National Park.

Local Food

At our in-house restaurant, we serve freshly prepared local Nepali dishes made with love and authentic ingredients. From traditional Thakali sets to spicy curries and seasonal vegetables, every meal offers a true taste of Nepal. Guests can enjoy both indoor and outdoor dining experiences, surrounded by a peaceful, natural atmosphere.

Park Activity

Located just minutes from Chitwan National Park, Hotel Safari offers easy access to a range of exciting activities. Guests can enjoy jungle safaris, canoe rides, bird watching, elephant encounters, and guided nature walks. Our experienced guides ensure your wildlife adventures are safe, informative, and unforgettable.
