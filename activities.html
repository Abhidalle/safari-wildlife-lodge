Activities

Elephant Ride

Traverse forest trails on a gentle elephant’s back. Our mahouts guide you safely through the jungle, offering close-up views of wildlife and lush surroundings.

Jungle Walk

Join an expert naturalist for a guided trek through Chitwan’s diverse ecosystem. Spot deer, birds, and other wildlife as you learn about native flora and fauna.

Canoe Trip

Glide along the Rapti River in a traditional wooden canoe. Observe crocodiles, water buffalo, and a variety of waterbirds in a serene riverine setting.

Jeep Safari

Embark on a thrilling open-top jeep ride through Chitwan National Park. Watch for rhinos, deer, and wild boar as you explore deeper into the protected reserve.

Elephant Bathing

Help bathe and scrub these majestic creatures in the river. Experience the special bond between mahouts and elephants while learning about their care.

Fishing Experience

Try your hand at traditional river fishing with bamboo rods and local bait. Our guides teach you the best spots and techniques along the riverbank.

Village Cycle Tour

Cycle through nearby Tharu villages, passing rice fields and homesteads. Visit local artisans, sample traditional snacks, and experience rural life firsthand.

Tharu Cultural Show

Evenings come alive with vibrant Tharu music, dance, and storytelling. Engage with performers and learn traditional steps in an authentic cultural showcase.

Bird Watching Tour

Rise before dawn and join our ornithologist-guided tour. Spot kingfishers, hornbills, and migratory species along forest edges and riverbanks.
