Our Services

Accommodation

Spacious, well-appointed rooms and suites designed for comfort and relaxation. Each unit features air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, private bathroom, and serene views of the gardens or pool.

Restaurant & Dining

Our on-site restaurant serves a variety of local Nepali specialties and international dishes made from fresh ingredients. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner options are available, with indoor and outdoor seating.

Room Service

Enjoy 24-hour room service for food and beverages. Choose from a wide menu of snacks, meals, and refreshments delivered right to your door, day or night.

Free Wi-Fi

Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the property, including guest rooms, lobby, restaurant, and outdoor areas. Stay connected with family or work seamlessly.

Spa & Wellness

Relax and rejuvenate with our spa treatments, including massages, facials, and aromatherapy. Our wellness center also features a sauna and steam room to help you unwind after a day of adventure.

Airport Pickup & Drop

Arrange convenient transportation to and from Bharatpur Airport. Our shuttle service ensures timely pickups and drop-offs, making your arrival and departure seamless.

Laundry & Dry Cleaning

Full laundry and dry-cleaning services are available with same-day turnaround. Drop off your garments and have them returned fresh and neatly pressed.

Conference & Event Space

Host meetings, seminars, or social events in our fully equipped conference hall. We provide audio-visual equipment, seating arrangements, and catering options to meet your needs.

Concierge & Tour Desk

Our concierge team can help you arrange local tours, jungle safaris, cultural experiences, and dining reservations. We’re here to ensure you get the most out of your stay in Chitwan.
