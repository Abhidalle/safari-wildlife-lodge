About Us

Gateway to the Jungle, Haven for the Soul.

Hotel Safari Resort is a peaceful retreat nestled near the edge of Chitwan National Park, offering guests a perfect balance of comfort and adventure. Surrounded by lush greenery and rich wildlife, our resort provides an ideal escape for nature lovers, families, and explorers seeking both relaxation and discovery. Whether you're watching the sunrise over the jungle or listening to birdsong from your balcony, every moment here connects you to the beauty of nature.

Our resort is designed to reflect the warmth of Nepali hospitality. With elegant rooms, attentive service, and traditional touches throughout, we ensure every guest feels at home. Enjoy delicious local and international cuisine at our restaurant, unwind in our garden lounge, or take part in cultural programs that showcase the rich heritage of the region. Every detail, from our décor to our service, is crafted to give you a truly authentic experience.

Beyond comfort, we offer unforgettable adventures. Guests can enjoy guided jungle safaris, canoe rides along the Rapti River, bird watching, and local village tours led by experienced naturalists. After a day of exploration, relax by the pool or gather around a bonfire under the stars. At Hotel Safari Resort, your journey into nature begins with a warm welcome and ends with lasting memories.

How to Get There

By Road: Hotel Safari Resort is easily accessible via the East-West Highway. From Bharatpur city, it's a short 15-minute drive toward Sauraha. Taxis, private vehicles, and buses are readily available.

By Air: The nearest airport is Bharatpur Airport, which offers regular flights from Kathmandu and Pokhara. From the airport, you can take a taxi or hotel-arranged pickup service to reach the resort.

By Bus: Regular tourist buses operate between Kathmandu, Pokhara, and Chitwan. Once you reach Sauraha Bus Park, Hotel Safari Resort is just a few minutes away by local transport or on foot.
